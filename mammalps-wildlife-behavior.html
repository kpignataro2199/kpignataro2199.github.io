Decoding Behavior with MammAlps

January 25, 2026

Researchers in the Swiss Alps have introduced MammAlps, a multimodal video dataset designed to train AI to better recognize wildlife behavior[cite: 52]. Unlike traditional camera traps that are difficult to analyze at scale, MammAlps uses audio and scene context to create ecologically meaningful training data[cite: 53, 54].

My take: This is game-changing for identifying behavior patterns that are easy to miss with the naked eye[cite: 59].
